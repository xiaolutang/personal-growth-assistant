### Changes

- 安装 openapi-fetch@0.17.0
- 创建 `createClient<paths>({ baseUrl: API_BASE })` 类型安全 client
- 新增 `handleOpenApiResponse` 统一错误处理函数
- CRUD 函数改用 client.GET/POST/PUT/DELETE：
  - getEntries: GET /entries
  - getEntry: GET /entries/{entry_id}
  - createEntry: POST /entries（含 type→category 映射）
  - updateEntry: PUT /entries/{entry_id}
  - deleteEntry: DELETE /entries/{entry_id}
  - getProjectProgress: GET /entries/{entry_id}/progress
- 搜索函数（searchEntries/vectorSearch/sqliteSearch）保留原生 fetch 不动
